The system

What lives inside

Four pillars that keep the shop honest. Every counter posts to the same ledger, so the number the biller sees is the number the accountant closes with — no evening reconciliation.

Billing that balances

Walk-in or on account, cash or cheque or split. Every bill writes stock, ledger and drawer in one transaction — edits reverse the whole thing before rewriting.

Stock you can trust

Every movement is a row: production, adjustment or sale. The per-product ledger shows the running balance and the low-stock bell rings the moment a shelf slips.

Cheques on a leash

Received, deposited, held or bounced — every status change moves the customer's balance the right way. A dashboard alert three days out means nothing matures forgotten.

Reports the boss reads

Sales by range, customer ledger, outstanding snapshot, petty cash by month. PDF for the file, Excel for the spreadsheet, same numbers as the screen.
